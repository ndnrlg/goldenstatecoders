A brief view into affordable healthcare Finding insurance that fits you

In the State of California, it is a requirement that people must have some form of health insurance. Otherwise they will be met with tax penalties. Although there are exemptions to these penalties, we want you to know that there is affordable healthcare out there that is accessible to people who have low income and are facing poverty. Let's take a look at some of them.

Getting the benefits you need California Health Insurances Guide

Medi-Cal is a public health insurance popular to Californians due to its little to no cost health coverage. There are many different ways to qualify for Medi-Cal including: having low-income, being at a certain age (21 or younger or 65 or older), being currently enrolled in other state programs such as CalFresh, and more. Medi-Cal helps cover the costs of seeing a doctor, going to the hopsital, dental care, surgery and more. There are 3 different ways to apply for this coverage:

  • Online
  • In person
    • Or
  • Call the service center that handles this insurance at (800) 300-1506.

    • Covered California is a free-to-use website that showcases you different health coverages and insurance benefits based on your income, location, and age. When putting this information into the Covered California calculator, it will return a free, estimated quote on what you could be paying for insurance. It will also point you to which of their plans you qualify for to get the most for your buck. Through this site, you will also be able to find other health coverages including dental and vision healthcare.

    It's important to note that as we grow older, our risk of health conditions also increases. This causes the cost of our health insurance to go up. However, it is always okay to ditch one insurance for another that better fits your needs and is within your budget. The cost of living in California is already high enough as it is, let's make it easier on us to find health coverage that not only helps us meet our needs, but it's also affordable.
